Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Open the app and follow the onboarding screens. No account or login is required to use the core features.

How do I restore a purchase?

Go to Settings in the app and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I cancel a subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone to cancel or manage your subscription.

The app is not working correctly

Try these steps: close and reopen the app; restart your device; check for app updates in the App Store. If the issue persists, email support@archaelnet.com with your device model and iOS version.

How do I delete my data?

Your data is stored locally on your device. Uninstall the app to delete all local data. For iCloud data, go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage.
